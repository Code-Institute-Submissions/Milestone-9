Damien Dempsey

☘️ Irish singer | songwriter ☘️

Damien Dempsey from Donaghmede, a Northside suburb of Dublin is an Irish singer and songwriter who mixes traditional Irish folk with contemporary lyrics to deliver social commentary on the positive and negative aspects arising from Ireland's Celtic Tiger society. He sings in English, and to a lesser extent in Irish.
