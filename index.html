Damien Dempsey

Damien Dempsey was born in 1975 in Donaghmede, Dublin. He is an Irish singer and songwriter who mixes traditional Irish folk with contemporary lyrics to deliver social commentary on the positive and negative aspects arising from Ireland's Celtic Tiger society. He sings in English, and to a lesser extent in Irish.
